ABOUT

Friends of Palo Alto Library Presents Used Book Sales!

    Our monthly sale includes over 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines, and more! Each sale allows us to continue to make space for incoming items and support the Library. Visit us each month to see the new items we have to offer.
These offerings are mostly from donations from individuals, estates, and companies. Our thanks goes out to our donors, library patrons, and YOU!

Rules:

    Our severe space limitations and compelling issues of health and safety require us to only allow 160 customers into the Main Room at a time.
    Due to number of customers on Saturdays there is a ticketed line system in place for the Main Room. (see FAQs for more info.)
